About the Site

ARCAL RLA 5/051 regional technical cooperation project brings together researchers from Latin America and Caribbean in the field of land degradation assessment through the measurement of environmental radionuclide inventories.

In that context, a system offering projectâ€™s participants the opportunity to disseminate their work and datasets, to compile relevant external datasets and to clearly visualize them, plays a critical role in improving decision making as regards national and regional policies for soil conservation and environmental protection in the region. Furthermore, the geographical dimension and cross-cutting nature of issues involved in ecosystem management requires deploying a system focused on strong geospatial visualization capabilities. This site is such a platform whose main objective is to serve as Geographic Decision Support System in the field of soil erosion monitoring and conservation.
